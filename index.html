What is Tea Trips?

Ever tried to plan a trip and you end up stressed with 10 tabs open looking between booking.com, google maps and noting it all down in word? TeaTrips tries to solve that problem by giving you all the tools you need in one place. You can search for places, add them to your trip, see them on a map and share your trip with friends (soon).

How long?

You can spend as long as you want on it. 10 minutes to spare? Anything helps and I greatly appreciate you giving up some time to make my site better!

What should I do?

First start by making an account and build up a user profile by saving locations. From there you can build itineraries either manually or generate personalised trips.
